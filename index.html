HOW TO USE

01 · Enter your name and pick Task 1 or Task 2. The timer and word target adjust automatically.

02 · Hit ▶ to start the timer. Write your essay — the word ring fills as you go (amber until you reach the target, green once you pass it).

03 · Use ⊡ Focus for a distraction-free exam view (Esc exits). Tap ▤ Structure for a quick template.

04 · Submit to cloud to save your essay to the Archive. You'll need the writing passcode the first time.

05 · In the Archive, open any essay to request AI feedback and an estimated band score, or download it as a PDF.

06 · Ask Pangeya AI anything about IELTS writing — structure, vocabulary, sample answers.
